Companion spatial imaging analysis

For multichannel IMC data spatial imaging analysis, we used the published TME Spatial pipeline. Use that repository to re-run or adapt the IMC spatial-analysis workflow, ROI analysis, cell distribution analysis, nearest-neighbor analysis, and distance analysis.

Zenodo data

The Zenodo record is listed as restricted access and contains processed mouse scRNA-seq matrices, per-sample and integrated Seurat/AnnData objects, sample metadata, IHC analysis files, IMC spatial-analysis files, and a human DCIS scRNA-seq reference dataset.
